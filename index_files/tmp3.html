Arriving in Ulanbaatar (UB)

If you’d like, we can help you make arrangements for lodging in UB, advise you on transportation from the airport or train station, and give you a private half-day or full-day city tour.  We are flexible and are here to help you as little or as much as you desire.  Please feel free to contact us for help when you are making your plans. 

It is especially nice to be oriented to the layout of the city before you go off to do exploring on your own.  We can show you where the major tourist sites are, teach you how to use the local transportation options,  take you to visit traditional restaurants, introduce you to our native foods, and teach you how to read our menus.

We can also take you on a tour of the ger district, the vast area of UB where most of the cities’ residents live.  Life here is very different from that in major cities in the West and sadly, most tourists never get an opportunity to see it and understand it.  Instead, they stay in the main tourist area because they don’t know how to access the ‘real’ Mongolia.  We can help to change that!

Ulanbaatar is a unique city full of interesting sights!  With a little help from a ‘local’, we can unlock some of its mysteries for you!  See the UB that most tourists miss!
