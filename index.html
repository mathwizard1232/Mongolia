Welcome to the home of TTNTours!  We are pleased that you found us!

We are a small tourist company serving the independent traveler in Mongolia.  Our desire is to share with you the real nomadic Mongolian way of life and the history of our people.  We want to introduce the great Chinggis Khaan’s country to you.

My name is Tsoogii.  I have worked with some of the largest tourist companies in Mongolia and now I want to offer my services directly to you.  I feel that in that way I can better share with you the essence of the real Mongolia.

Please see our “About Me” and “Testimonial” pages for more information about us and how we came to start this company.

We would like to help you with your travel needs while in Mongolia.  We are a small, independent company and so we are able to be flexible and plan a trip around your personal needs, whether you are traveling singly or with a group.

We are delighted that you are interested in visiting Mongolia!  Please feel free to contact us with your questions and requests.
