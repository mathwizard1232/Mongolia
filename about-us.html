Tsoogii and Turbold

My name is Chimedtsogzol S. My family and friends call me Tsoogii. I grew up in the sum (town) of Erdenekhairkhan in Zavkhan aimag (province), which is in the north central portion of Mongolia.

I teach English at the National University of Mongolia. I am currently completing the requirements for my PhD in English.

I live in Ulanbaatar, which is the capital of Mongolia. For the past four years, I have been guiding with some of the largest tourist companies in Mongolia. During that time I have become familiar with all of the areas that are favorites for tourists to visit.

In 2009, my husband, Boroo, and I opened TTNTours in order to offer services to the independent traveler.

Our company name represents the initials of the members of my family:

T — my son, Turuu

T — my son, Turbold

N — my husband, Nergui (known also as Boroo)

T — myself, Tsoogii

It is our goal to show you the real Mongolian nomadic lifestyle and also to introduce you to the country of the great Chinggis Khaan.
